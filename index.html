Initiative Visualization

This course is designed for experts in the fields of Occupational Safety and Health (OSH), labor inspection, social protection, social dialogue, and labor migration. It provides participants with essential knowledge on the subject of digital governance.

Our Value Proposition: We believe digital governance is the ability to manage the core elements that make up the digital world. We have identified six key elements that are always present and interacting within any digital environment: Purpose, Data, Tools, Methodology, People, and Processes. Whether at a territorial, organizational, or even personal level, these six elements exist in varying degrees of intensity or maturity.
